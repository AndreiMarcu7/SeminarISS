The man had dark green eyes and a big smile on his face. He was wearing a black suit and a red tie. He looked like he was waiting for someone. For her.
She was scared but also curious. She wanted to know who he was and why he was there. She decided to approach him and ask him.
As she got closer and closer to him, she felt her heart pounding like never before. 
She recognised him from the fair she had been to a few days ago. He was the magician who had performed some amazing tricks. She had been mesmerized by his performance and had wanted to talk to him ever since. Now, here he was, standing in front of her, looking even more charming than before.
She gathered all her courage and said: "Hi, I remember you from the fair. You were amazing! I wanted to talk to you but I was too shy. Can we talk now?"
